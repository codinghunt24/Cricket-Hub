What is ads.txt?

ads.txt (Authorized Digital Sellers) Google AdSense ke liye ek zaroori file hai jo confirm karti hai ki aap apni website ke authorized seller hain. Iske bina AdSense revenue kam ho sakta hai ya ads show nahi hote.

Format: google.com, pub-XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX, DIRECT, f08c47fec0942fa0

Google AdSense ke liye ads.txt kaise setup karein

  1. AdSense Dashboard par jaayein
  2. Account → Sites mein apni site check karein
  3. Warning show hogi: "Earnings at risk – You need to fix some ads.txt file issues"
  4. Wahan diya gaya line copy karein — format hoga:
    google.com, pub-XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX, DIRECT, f08c47fec0942fa0
  5. Upar text area mein paste karein aur Save karein
  6. Google 24 ghante mein verify kar leta hai
